This laptop is overall $750 dollars. It has very good specs for the price.
The i7 can handle 1080p rendering, but the ssd s slow write speed might slow it down.

Build quality is very good. Full aluminum.
Screen is a 1080p ips panel with infinity edge display. The webcam is 720p and is decent.
Keyboard is great. One of the best for this price. The trackpad is not so great. Elan drivers and imperfect tracking.

It has an intel hd 520 OR iris 540 that can handel low to medium demanding games.
8gb of ddr3 ram, it's the sweet spot for normal use.

The heat is fine. Nothing too bad, the speakers are good.
Loud, nice bass, on the bottom, but it isn't particulary clear.

Unfortunately, I wish they gave us better ports.
In this model, they give us mini hdmi, and 2 usb 2.0 and an ssd along with the microphone and headphone jack.
